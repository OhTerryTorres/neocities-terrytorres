My brothers, Tim and Brendan, and I got together a while ago to review The 3rd Birthday, Square Enix’s worst ever treaty violation.<\/p>\n

It was our first night together since Christmastime, so we were excited AS HELL to talk about this, and well, we started recording around 10 at night and we stopped around 2 in the morning. We cover the game with a fine-tooth comb from beginning to end, with plenty of (non-boring) tangents related to many other games (Resident Evil, Mass Effect, Illusion of Gaia, etc.) and ideas (sci-fi and art, etc.).<\/p>\n

It’s in .mp3 format, split apart into four segments for palatable listening.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

3rd Birthday Stinks and We Don’t Like It<\/h1>\n

Part One: 3rd Birthday Stinks and We Don’t Like It<\/a>
Some Parasite Eve 1 talk and a lot about 3rd Birthday’s premise and setting.
Spoiler Level: Low <\/p>\n

Part Two: The Greatest Foe Lies Within (Bad Games)<\/a>
All the gameplay and the entire plot up to the ending gets dissected.
Spoiler Level: HUUUGE<\/p>\n

Part Three: It Was the Best of Time Zero, It Was the Blurst of Time Zero<\/a>
The entire ending under the microscope.
Spoiler Level: Monumental, and not just for The 3rd Birthday. PE1, PE2 and even Chrono Cross get spoiled.<\/p>\n

Part Four: One More Final: I Need You (To Make Good Games Again)<\/a>
Final thoughts, a lot of talk about recent and past Square games.
